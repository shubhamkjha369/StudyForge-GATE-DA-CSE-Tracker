# StudyForge — Master Exam Tracker (GATE DA Edition) 🚀

StudyForge is a premium, self-contained study dashboard designed specifically for **GATE Data Science & AI (DA)** aspirants. It combines productivity tools, academic tracking, and gamification to create an immersive study experience.

## ✨ Key Features

### 📅 Roadmap & Syllabus Tracker
*   **Multi-Phase Structure**: Organizes subjects into logical phases (Phase 1: Math & Fundamentals, Phase 2: Core DA, Phase 3: Advanced Topics).
*   **Granular Tracking**: Mark subjects and individual subtopics as complete.
*   **Mastery Levels**: Track your understanding of topics using a color-coded mastery system (Struggling, Comfortable, Mastered).

### ⏳ Advanced Pomodoro System
*   **Customizable Intervals**: Set your own work and break times.
*   **Subject Sync**: Link your focus session to a specific subject from the syllabus.
*   **Visual Ring**: Aesthetic progress ring with session counting.
*   **Audio Oasis**: Integrated Lo-fi, Rain, and Forest ambient sounds to aid concentration.

### 🎮 Gamified Experience (RPG)
*   **Leveling System**: Earn XP by completing topics and finishing study sessions.
*   **XP Progress Bar**: Visual feedback on your growth from "Novice" to "Grandmaster Scholar."
*   **Coins & Shop**: Earn coins and spend them in the "Forge Shop" for streak shields and milestones.
*   **Streak Badges**: Keep your momentum alive with fire streaks.

### 📊 Powerful Analytics
*   **Study Heatmap**: GitHub-style contribution chart showing your study consistency over the last year.
*   **Mock Test Hub**: Log mock test results and visualize your performance trend with a custom SVG chart.
*   **Progress Insights**: AI-style alerts showing neglected subjects or mock performance warnings.

### 🧠 Expert Utility Tools
*   **SRS Flashcards**: Integrated flashcard system using the **SM-2 algorithm** for spaced repetition.
*   **GATE Virtual Calculator**: A draggable, fully functional scientific calculator matching the GATE interface.
*   **Global Search**: Quickly jump to any topic across the massive DA syllabus.
*   **Study Logs**: Detailed history of all your recorded sessions.

### 🎨 Premium Aesthetics
*   **Dynamic Themes**:
    *   🏜️ **Desert**: A warm, paper-like aesthetic for daytime focus.
    *   🌑 **Deep Dark**: A sleek, high-contrast dark mode for night owls.
    *   ⚡ **Cyber Neon**: A vibrant, glowing cyberpunk theme for high-energy sessions.
*   **Responsive Design**: Works beautifully on desktops and tablets.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
*   **Frontend**: Vanilla HTML5, CSS3 (Custom Variables, Flexbox, Grid)
*   **Logic**: Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+)
*   **Storage**: `localStorage` (No server needed, your data stays on your machine)
*   **Fonts**: Sora, JetBrains Mono, Fraunces (via Google Fonts)

## 🚀 Getting Started
Since StudyForge is a self-contained application, there's no installation required!

1.  Clone the repository:
    ```bash
    git clone https://github.com/your-username/studyforge-gate-da.git
    ```
2.  Open `StudyForge_Tracker_V2.html` in any modern web browser (Edge, Chrome, or Firefox).
3.  **Optional**: Pin the tab or add it to your bookmarks for quick access.

## 💾 Data Management
*   **Self-Hosting**: Your data is stored locally in your browser's `localStorage`.
*   **Export/Import**: Found in the settings menu, allowing you to move your progress between browsers or create backups.
*   **Sync**: Supports cross-tab synchronization (if you project the tracker across multiple windows).
